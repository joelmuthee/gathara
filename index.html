About Us

Four Decades of Engineering Excellence

Founded in 1984 by Principal Engineer Eng. James Gathara, our firm has grown into one of Nairobi's most trusted civil and structural engineering consultancies. Our track record spans residential, commercial, institutional, and infrastructure projects across Kenya.

We pair decades of on-the-ground experience with modern engineering tools — so every structure we stamp stands for generations.

  • Authorized to practice by the Engineers Board of Kenya
  • Comprehensive in-house capability across all disciplines
  • Projects from site investigation through to structural completion
  • On-time delivery and transparent client communication
